Elephant Electrocuted by a High Tention Wire (India)
New Indian Express
July 7, 2015

BELAGAVI: A 9-year-old tusker was electrocuted after it came in contact
with a high tension wire near Nersa village in Khanapur taluk on Sunday.

According to villagers, the male elephant was walking on the border of a
field when it slipped. It grabbed the wire with its trunk, getting
electrocuted and dying on the spot. The tusker was accompanied by a female
elephant, which panicked and fled into the jungles. Forest officials found
