Wild elephant found dead in Jhapa (Nepal)
Rastriya Samachar Samiti, Himalayan Times
May 7, 2016

KAKARBHITTA:  A baby wild elephant was found dead at Thoplebiran Community
Forest in Mechinagar-4 of Jhapa district on Saturday morning.

The 10-year-old male elephant died due to electric shock, the District
Forest Office, Morang informed.

A joint team of the District Police Office, Morang and the District Forest
Office in the district reached the spot and confirmed the death of wild
elephant.

Last year, another wild elephant was found dead at Bahunidanda in the
district.

More than two dozen of wild elephants were reported dead in various places
in Jhapa and Morang district since 11 years
