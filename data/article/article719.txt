Carcasses of two elephants found by forest officials (State of Tamil Nadu,
India)
The Press Trust of India
June 3, 2018

The carcasses of two elephants were found by forest officials in the
district today, police said.

They said the carcass of a one-and-a-half-year old bull elephant was found
in a nearby slushy area.

Veterinarians conducted a postmortem and buried the animal near the spot.

A 15-year-old cow elephant died after it slipped and fell off a rocky
outcrop at Gudalur in Nilgiris district, they said.

The elephant had strayed into the town last night, damaged a wall and fell
to a depth of 30 feet, resulting in fatal injuries, police said.
