Female elephant electrocuted in Puwakpitiya (Sri Lanka)
News 1st
October 2, 2018

See link
<https://www.newsfirst.lk/2018/10/02/female-elephant-electrocuted-in-puwakpitiya/>
for video.

COLOMBO: A female elephant died after coming in to contact with an
unprotected electricity cable in Puwakpitiya, Habarana last afternoon
(October 1).

The News 1st correspondent in the area said the elephant had roamed into
the area from the Hurulu Eco park.

Wildlife Officers said the elephant had come in to contact with an
electricity cable that was set up for the protection of crops.

Investigations into the incident are underway.
