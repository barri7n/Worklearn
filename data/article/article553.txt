COIMBATORE: An elephant was found electrocuted on a farmland in the
Sirumugai forest range in the district today.

Forest officials noticed the carcass near an electric fence on the land
belonging to one Manickam, forest department sources said.

The jumbo appeared to be 15-years-old, police said, adding, a search is on
for Manickam for illegally putting up an electric fence.
