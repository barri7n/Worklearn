Elephant electrocuted (Assam, India)
Press Trust of India
November 30, 2016

Barpeta (Assam), Nov 30 (PTI) A male adult elephant was electrocuted to
death after coming into contact with a transformer at nearby Dihir Balapara
area in Barpeta district today, said a forest official here.

The 30-year-old elephant had come out of the Manas National Park towards
Kokilabari and got electrocuted from the transformer which is near a road,
said Hiranta Sarma, Manas Tiger project Director.

The transformer was lying on the ground, Sarma said.
