Poachers kill elephant in Orissa forest reserve (India)
IBN Live
January 27,2011

Rourkela (Orissa), Jan 27 (PTI) A decomposed carcass of an elephant was found today at Kunjur reserve forest in Sundergarh district. The dead elephant's tusk is missing, DFO Arun Mishra said adding that it is suspected that poachers have killed the elephant and escaped with the tusk. However, the exact cause of death of the pachyderm will be known after postmortem, he said.
