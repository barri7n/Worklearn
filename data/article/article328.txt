Tusker found dead (Nepal)
The Himalayan
November 21, 2014

BIRGUNJ: A wild elephant has been found dead in Madhuban Mathawal of Parsa
on Friday morning.

The tusker was found dead nearby Rangapur Range Post located at Gadi VDC
which is one kilometer far from Madhuban Mathawal-5, Akuniya in the west.

The elephant is said to have received bullets on its body. It is suspected
that the elephant was shot dead by poacher.
