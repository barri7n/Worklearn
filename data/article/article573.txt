Madikeri: Deaths of two wild elephants have reported at Kannagala village
of Virajapet taluk in this district.

Forest officials said two female elephants aged 26 and 19 years died, while
grazing in a private estate. According to report the elephants pulled a
tree and the branch touched the power lines resulting in their
electrocution.

According to a villager four elephants have died in the district recently
due to various reasons, while one injured elephant taken shelter in a pond
died due to wounds, other was dead when it got stuck in the mud.

A disappointed villager narrated that the human and animal conflict was
increasing due to insufficient permanent measures taken by forest
department and elephants are riding private estates and killing workers.
