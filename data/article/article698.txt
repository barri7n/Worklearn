Tusker dies of electrocution (State of Odisha, India)
Web India 123
February 12, 2018

A 25 year old tusker died of electrocution at a jungle near sanakanthkula
under Bantal section of Angul forest division on Monday Forest department
sources said the animal died after coming into contact with live wire
spread in that area for poaching.

The body has been sent for post mortem.

Divisional Forest Officer V Kartikeyan who rushed to the spot said action
will be initiated on the basis of the post mortem report. The tusker was a
lone one and the tusk was intact.
