25 Year-Old Male Elephant Electrocuted (Mysore District, India)
The Shillong Times
August 23, 2017

Mysuru: A 25-year-old male elephant died of electrocution when it came in
contact live electric fencing at Narasipur village of H D Kote taluk in the
district.

Police said the elephant which came from the nearby forest in search of
food tried to enter the Farm of Naveena when it came in contact with the
fencing which was illegally electrified, the elephant died instantly.

Saraguru forest officials have rushed to the spot.

A complaint has been registered against the farm owner.
