Female Elephant Electrocuted in Nilgiris District (India)
New Indian Express
18th June 2015

UDHGAMANDALAM: A female elephant was found electrocuted on the fence of a
coffee estate at Nilakottai in Nilgiris district today, forest department
officials said.
The elephant was noticed by some villagers who informed the Forest
Department.
A herd of elephants, numbering eight, along with a calf, have surrounded
the dead elephant, they said.
