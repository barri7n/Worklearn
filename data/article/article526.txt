Injured tusker gets treatment, food (Sri Lanka)
Daily Mirror
March 21, 2017

See link
<http://www.dailymirror.lk/article/Injured-tusker-gets-treatment-food--125893.html>
for photos.

Wildlife officials were seen working their fingers to the bone to save an
injured tusker lying down helplessly by the Adiyakadawala tank in
Kottukachchiya.

Dr. Isuru Hewa Kottage said the tusker?s health condition was serious but
they were doing everything they can to save him.

Meanwhile, people were seen visiting the location carrying fruits to feed
the helpless tusker.
