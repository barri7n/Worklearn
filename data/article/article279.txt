Carcass of Jumbo found (India)

By Express News Service 

12th November 2013



Forest officials on Monday found the decomposed body of a wild elephant with its tusk missing from Kholgarh Reserve forest near Gambhariberni village under Rampur Forest Range.



Investigation revealed that the tusker was 50-year-old and bore no injury marks. It could have been poisoned by poachers for the tusk. On being informed, Assistant Conservator of Forest (Territorial), Rairakhol, S C Sahu went to the spot and autopsy was performed on the elephant.
