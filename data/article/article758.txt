Tusker found dead in Bolangir (State of Odisha, India)
The Orissa Post
September 19, 2018

See link <http://www.orissapost.com/tusker-found-dead-in-bolangir/> for
photo.

BOLANGIR: A wild tusker was found dead at Uparajhara village under Deogaon
block in Bolangir district Wednesday.

The elephant was reportedly electrocuted after coming in contact with a
live 11KV wire.

Sources said the wild pachyderm might have come to the area in search of
food.

The local residents also alleged that two tusks of the jumbo have been
removed by miscreants.

On being informed, the forest officials reached the spot and started an
investigation.
