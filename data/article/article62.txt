Elephant electrocuted (India) 
The Statesman
November 25, 2007

PARALAKHEMUNDI, Nov. 25: An elephant which had escaped from the Lakhari forests along with a herd and crossed the Andhra Pradesh border died due to electrocution. This incident happened at the Jirayat Gopalapuram village near Beeraghatam in the Vizianagaram district, 70 km from here.
The female adult tusker was trying to enter a paddy field, when she came in contact with a low electric cable and was killed.It may be noted here that a herd from Lakhari forests here had crossed over AP and caused mayhem there. Operation Gajendra was launched to push the herd back to Lakhari.The operation created an uproar when one of the two elephants which were tranquilised died.
