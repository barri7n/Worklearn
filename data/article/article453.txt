Fifteen-Year-Old Tusker Electrocuted (Udhagamandalam, India)
Press Trust of India
June 15, 2016

A 15-year-old tusker was electrocuted when it came into contact with an
illegal electric fence of a private coffee estate near here.

A group of estate workers today noticed the elephant lying dead on the
fence, which was allegedly connected to high voltage electricity, forest
department sources said.

Officials from forest department and electricity board rushed to the spot,
along with two veterinarians, Prabhu and Kaliyappan, who conducted the post
mortem and buried the male elephant, they said.

Police said they were on the look out for the estate owners as it was an
offence to put up high voltage electric fences.
