
A massive elephant in Galgamuwa has been shot dead by a person. The
Wildlife Conservation ofcials suspect that the elephant has been shot by a
hunter. The elephant has been shot on the 10 and the dead body has been
found later. According to the Wildlife Conservation Department, the
elephant is of 8 feet height and of 40 years old.  Post mortem of the
elephant was held by the Veterinary of Anuradhapura-Pandulagama Veterinary
unit and it was revealed that the death has occurred due to being shot.
