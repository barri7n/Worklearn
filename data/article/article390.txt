Carcass of elephant calf recovered (India, Odisha)
Odisha Sun Times Bureau
November 10, 2015

Forest department officials today recovered a carcass of an elephant calf
from Ganesh Khol forested area under Sarangi range in Odishaâ€™s Dhenkanal
district.

Two persons have been injured by the mother of the dead calf.

The injured are the local forester Sagramani and a photographer.

The two were injured when they were attacked by the mother elephant that
was guarding her dead calf.

Though reason behind the death of the two-month old calf is not yet clear,
the local DFO has stated that the cause of the death will be ascertained
after the post-mortem is done.
