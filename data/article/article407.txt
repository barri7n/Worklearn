Elephant Electrocuted in Pathanamthitta Borders, Dead (India, Kerala)
By Express News Service
28th December 2015

KOLLAM: An elephant was found electrocuted in the forest bordering
Pathanamthitta, forest sources said on Sunday. The same elephant had
trampled to death a forester about a year ago. The 45-year-old elephant is
suspected to have been electrocuted by a 220 kv power line. The Forest
Department has registered a case against the KSEB in this regard.
