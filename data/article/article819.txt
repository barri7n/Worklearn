Elephant electrocuted in C'garh (State of Chhattisgarh, India)
The Press Trust of India
December 19, 2018

An elephant was electrocuted after it accidentally came in contact with a
live wire at a farm in Raigarh district of Chhattisgarh, forest officials
said on Wednesday.

The carcass of the 15-year-old male pachyderm was found Wednesday in the
field located in Purunga village in Chhal forest range, Dharamjaigarh
Divisional Forest Official Pranay Mishra said.
