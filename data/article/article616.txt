Wild elephant died due to bleeding: report (Thrissur District, India)
The Hindu
September 14, 2017

A she elephant that was found dead in a check-dam in Nayattukundu forest,
near Kodakara, two days ago died due to internal bleeding, according to a
post-mortem report.

According to the report submitted by senior veterinary officer Jayakumar,
the 40-year-old elephant, which was pregnant, suffered bleeding from the
uterus.

Body found

Body of the elephant was found in the Ambazhakundu check-dam in the forest
area.

Forest officials found the body while patrolling the area.

The body of the elephant was buried in the forest after the post-mortem.
