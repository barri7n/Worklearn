Human-Elephant Conflict (Sri Lanka)
Daily Mirror 
April 5, 2011

A wild elephant died from the severe injuries caused by Hakka Patas an explosive used in illegal hunting. Wildlife Conservation Officers said the elephant that had strayed into the Kinniya village had swallowed the explosive device laid by the poachers to kill wild boar. The Wildlife Officials who rushed to the scene when informed by the villagers and treated the elephant that had suffered a broken jaw and was bleeding profusely from the mouth. However it was too late for another majestic elephant.
