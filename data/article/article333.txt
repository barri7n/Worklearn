Injured Tusker Dies (India)
Express News Service
November 29, 2014

DHENKANAL:  A 15-year-old tusker that had sustained bullet injuries
succumbed at Kandar under Kamakshyanagar block on Friday.

Forest officials said the tusker was shot by poachers in Jajpur and brought
to Kandar for specialised treatment. Dhenkanal DFO PK Sahoo said the
elephant could not walk properly and was being administered medicines for
the last three days. Its condition was serious despite treatment and
succumbed, he added.

In the last seven days, three elephants have died. Two of them were
electrocuted in Hindol forest range. According to official reports, 11
elephants have died in eight months while seven were killed by poachers
since April.
