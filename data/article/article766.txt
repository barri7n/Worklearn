Injured elephant discovered in Panwewa forest reserve (Sri Lanka)
News 1st
October 1, 2018

See link
<https://www.newsfirst.lk/2018/10/01/injured-elephant-discovered-in-panwewa-forest-reserve/>
for video.

COLOMBO: Following a search operation that was carried out by Hambantota
wildlife officers, an injured elephant was discovered in the Panwewa forest
reserve in Hambanthota.

The wildlife officers confirmed that the elephant had sustained injuries on
his left hind leg due to a gun shot.

The News 1st correspondent in the area stated that the elephant will be
taken in for treatment today (October 1) by the Udawalawa wildlife
veterinarian officer.
