Wild jumbo dies in sanctuary (Thailand)
Bangkok Post
June 23, 2012

KANCHANABURI: A wild elephant was found dead in Si Sawat district in the Salak Phra Wildlife Sanctuary yesterday. Veterinarians said the elephant may have been killed by an electric shock from the fence around the orchard or by poisonous food it had eaten.

The 20-year-old male elephant weighing about 2 tonnes was found dead near an electric fence at a fruit orchard in Ban Mong Katae.
