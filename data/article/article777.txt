Elephant dies in motor accident (Sri Lanka)
News 1st
October 25, 2018

See link
<https://www.newsfirst.lk/2018/10/25/elephant-dies-in-motor-accident/> for
photo.

COLOMBO: A wild elephant died last night (October 24) after it was knocked
by a van in Mahamorakanda along the Habarana-Trincomalee road. The van was
damaged, but no one was injured.

Meanwhile, the report of the committee appointed to look into the wild
elephant deaths along the rail routes will be handed over to Minister of
Transport and Civil Aviation Nimal Siripala de Silva today (October 25).
