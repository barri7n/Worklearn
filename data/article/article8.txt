2 elephants killed in Athmallik
Orissa.net
April 5, 2004

A tusker was killed by poachers in Nuagana forest under Athmallik forest 
range on Friday. Official sources said the body of the tusker (45) with 
bullet injuries was found at Gambhir Chap.

Conservator of Forests, Angul circle, Nagraj Reddy said raids are on at 
different places to nab the culprits. This is the second tusker killed in 
the area within a week. On March 29, a young tusker was found dead at 
Dhobamunda jungle under Madhapur range.
