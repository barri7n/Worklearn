Elephant electrocuted in Erode (State of Tamil Nadu, India)
The Hindu
September 3, 2018

A male elephant aged 35 years was found electrocuted in a farm land’s
electric fence at the Karumbarai in Anthiyur forest area here.

Forest officials said the search was on for Kothandaraman, 56, the land’s
owner and he will be booked for the illegal fence.

K. Ashokan, wild life veterinarian of Sathyamangalam Tiger Reserve,
conducted the post-mortem.
