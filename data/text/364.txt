PRACHUAP KHIRI KHAN - The manager of a farm close to the Kaeng Krachan National Park has confessed to killing three wild elephants which were found dead in a district of Hua Hin on Wednesday. INN reported... The dead elephants were a male aged about 10 years with 30-centimetre-long tusks, a female 6-7 years old, and a female juvenile aged 3-4 years.
