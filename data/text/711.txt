COLOMBO: An elephant was found dead in the Ambagaswewa tank in Palagala,
Anuradhapura.

According to villagers, the elephant had died this morning (May 2nd).

Several bullets were found in the remains of the elephant.

The injuries in its mouth area indicate that the elephant had bitten into
hakka patas (An explosives used to scare away elephants).
