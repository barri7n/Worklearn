Elephant found dead (Udhagamandalam, India)
The Hindu
January 7, 2017

Another elephant was found dead less than a 100 metres from the Moyar
River, near Thengumarada, in the Nilgiris on Friday. This is the second
elephant in the last month that has been found dead close to the Moyar.

Speaking to The Hindu , V Selvam, Forest Range Officer, said that the
female elephant was found dead in Thengumarada section of Mangalapatti beat
in Nilgiris Eastern range on Friday.

Forest Department officials said that a post-mortem was conducted on the
animal. In a separate, unrelated incident, a male gaur was found dead near
Nedugula, in Kattabettu Range, in the Nilgiris on Friday.
