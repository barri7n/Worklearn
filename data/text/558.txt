SAMBALPUR: THE carcass of a female elephant was found in Haldi village
under Rengali forest range in the district on Sunday. According to sources,
the villagers spotted the carcass in a mango orchard and informed the
forest officials, who rushed to the spot and started investigation. Rengali
Forest Range Officer Harihar Patra said the age of the elephant is around
12 years.

Sunstroke is suspected to be the reason behind its death but the actual
cause can be ascertained only after the autopsy report is received, he
said.The carcass was buried at the spot after autopsy, he added.
