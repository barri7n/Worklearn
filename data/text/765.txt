Tusker found dead (State of Karnataka, India)
The Hindu
October 2, 2018

A tusker was found dead at Beeranahalli in Tarikere taluk on Monday. The
animal is believed to have died after it came in contact with a live wire
in an agriculture field.

Lakkavalli Range Forest officer Chandrashekhar Reddy and his staff reached
the spot after the local residents informed them about the incident.

Veterinarians conducted the post-mortem and burnt the carcass later.

The officers said they would investigate the case and arrest those
responsible for the death of the animal.
