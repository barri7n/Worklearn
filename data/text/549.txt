A 25-year-old tusker was found dead on a farm land at Dasaripalli in Thally
here on Saturday. The elephant had injuries on its trunk.
The elephant was among a group of four elephants that had entered
Dasaripalli on Friday night. However, one of the elephants was found dead
on the field of a farmers in the morning. The tusker had frothed in the
mouth and had injuries on its trunk. Sources said that pus had oozed from
his left and right rear limbs.

According to Forest Department veterinarian Prakash, the cause of death
suspected was electrocution. However, asked if there was an electric line,
he said there was a line 400 meters from the carcass, and also in its
vicinity.
