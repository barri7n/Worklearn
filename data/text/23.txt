Elephant found dead
Newindpress.com
Wednesday November 9 2005

BODI: A wild elephant was found dead at a plantation near Thevaram on 
Sunday.

Wild animals, especially elephants, from the jungles of the Cumbum valley 
enter the plantations and destroy crops.

The carcass of the wild elephant was recovered by the Forest Department 
officials of Uthamapalayam and the wildlife warden.

A postmortem revealed that the pachyderm had died of electrocution.

Officials said that the animal had come into contact with an electric fence 
erected by one Suruli Thevar around his plantation.

Forest department officers arrested Suruli Thevar for enquiry.
