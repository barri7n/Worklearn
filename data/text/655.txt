Elephant foun d dead (State of Tamil Nadu, India)
The New Indian Express
November 9, 2017

COIMBATORE: An elephant was found dead outside an agricultural land on the
outskirts today.

The villagers noticed the carcass of the female elephant and immediately
informed forest department officials, who rushed to the spot coming under
Madukkarai forest range.

The officials recovered the carcass and sent the vital organs for autopsy,
which would help reveal the cause of death, department sources said.
