Live Wire Kills Three Elephants in Orissa (India) 

Telugu Portal

October 18, 2006

Bhubaneswar, Oct 18 (IANS) Three elephants were killed in Orissa's Sambalpur district when one of them got caught in a live wire and the other two tried to rescue it.

The adult male and two young female elephants were killed instantly near Girischandrapur village early Tuesday, said state wildlife chief S.C. Mohanty.

Six days ago, two elephants were found dead in a paddy field in the northern district of Keonjhar. The reason for their deaths is not known.
