Elephant found dead in Corbett (Nainital District, India)
The Tribune
August 27, 2017

Dehradun: A female elephant was found dead in Corbett Tiger Reserve's Dhela
range on Friday.

The body of the elephant was found by members of the patrolling team of the
reserve.

Corbett director Surendra Mehra ruled out any foul play, as all body parts
of the elephant were intact. Experts said the cause of the death was a
fight between elephants.
